WET PETS AQUARIUM REBRAND

My current redesign for a local aquarium located in Milton, Ontario. Through this project I worked to create a new logo, typeface, packaging, and business cards. The new logo is
designed as a word mark, a pictorial logo, and a combination
of the two. Lastly, I worked to redesign the overall visual
aesthetic of the company, giving suggestions of a new
colour pallets that stray away from the previous neon
blues and yellows. I further worked in illustrator to
design the coral and bubble pattern that is present
throughout the rebrand, found on the packaging,
business card, and the board as a whole.

02

CREATIVE DIGITAL
RESUME

This project was completed in my Grade 12 media design
class. The goal was to create a digital resume that
showcased our design skills while offering a quick glimpse
into our interests and identities as designers. Although the
project was primarily for fun, it taught me valuable
skills in Illustrator, such as creating icons, working
with textures, and designing avatars. Through this
project, I also learned the importance of visual
storytelling and consistency in design, ensuring that
each element on the resume reflected my personal
style and professional brand.
