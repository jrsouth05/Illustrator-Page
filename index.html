WET PETS AQUARIUM REBRAND

My current redesign for a local aquarium located in Milton, Ontario. Through this project I worked to create a new logo, typeface, packaging, and business cards. The new logo is designed as a word mark, a pictorial logo, and a combination of the two. Lastly, I worked to redesign the overall visual aesthetic of the company, giving suggestions of a new colour pallets that stray away from the previous neon blues and yellows.
